Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Still unsure which package fits your move? Call or text us and we will help.

How many totes do I need?

Our 20-tote package generally works for a studio or one-bedroom move, 40 totes for two to three bedrooms, and 60 totes for larger homes. Every move is different, so we can help you choose.

What if I need additional totes?

Additional totes are available for $7 each, subject to availability.

Are the totes cleaned between customers?

Yes. Totes, lids and dollies are cleaned and sanitized after each rental.

Can I extend my rental?

Yes. Flexible extensions are available. Contact us as early as possible so we can confirm inventory and scheduling.

Where do you deliver?

Delivery and pickup are included in Lamar, Pike, Spalding, Butts, Upson and Monroe counties. Other Middle Georgia areas are available for a small travel fee.

Do packages include dollies?

Yes. The 20-tote package includes one, the 40-tote package includes two, and the 60-tote package includes three.

Do I need to clean the totes before pickup?

We ask that totes be emptied and free of personal items or loose debris. We handle the full cleaning and sanitizing process after pickup.

How do I reserve my dates?

Submit the booking form, call or text us. Your dates are not confirmed until we follow up and finalize the reservation.
