Our Story

Built by a family who understands moving.

Meet the Weavers

More than a rental company.

We're the Weavers—a busy family who knows firsthand how stressful moving can be. That is exactly why we started Weaver Tote Co.: to make moving a little easier and more organized for other families in our community.

The big kids help load and unload totes, everyone pitches in to clean and sanitize between rentals, and the littles are always happy to be our unofficial marketing team.

We are building something our family can be proud of while providing a service our neighbors can genuinely use.

We proudly serve Lamar, Pike, Spalding, Butts, Upson and Monroe counties with free delivery, plus other Middle Georgia communities for a small travel fee.
