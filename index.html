About Us

Family owned, locally run

Weaver Tote Co. started with a simple idea: moving shouldn't mean a garage full of soggy cardboard and a roll of tape you'll never use again. We deliver clean, sturdy totes and rolling dollies right to your door.

We proudly serve Lamar, Pike, Spalding, Butts, Upson, and Monroe counties with free delivery, plus the rest of middle Georgia for a small travel fee.
